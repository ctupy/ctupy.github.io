Christine's Intresting Topic

Since Halloween is coming up, I have decided to talk about the Winchester Mystery House. After her husband died, Sarah Winchester was told by a medium that she is cursed and haunted by those killed by the Winchester rifles. She was told that in order to evade the angry spirits, she would have to "move out west, buy a home, and build nonstop." Another legend states that if she would stop building, she would die. Due to the mansion being built to house countless spirits, it is considered one of the most haunted houses in the world.
